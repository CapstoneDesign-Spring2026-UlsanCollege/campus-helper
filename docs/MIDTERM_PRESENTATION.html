Midterm Presentation
Problem
Slide 2 / 10

What problem are we solving?

Students often need to jump between different systems for schedules, academic resources, communication, announcements, and campus help. That friction wastes time and makes campus life harder to manage.

Why it matters

  • Important information is scattered.
  • Students lose time switching between tools.
  • Support and communication are inconsistent.
  • New students have a harder time navigating campus workflows.
Clear, centralized student workflow
User
Slide 3 / 10

Primary users

  • Ulsan College students
  • New students unfamiliar with campus workflows
  • Student communities needing resource sharing and chat

Secondary users

  • Faculty or staff who need to publish notices
  • Admins managing users and campus announcements
We designed for real student tasks, not a generic portal
Solution
Slide 4 / 10

Campus Helper does this

  • Authenticates students and admins
  • Provides a central dashboard
  • Offers an AI assistant with saved history
  • Supports schedules, notes, chat, and campus services

Core modules

  • AI Assistant
  • Timetable
  • Notes
  • Marketplace
  • Lost & Found
  • Network and Direct Chat
  • Admin Notices
One authenticated campus command center
