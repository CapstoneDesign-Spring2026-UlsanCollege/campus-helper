🏢 Finding Your Classroom

Example: Your class is in A503

  1. First digit (5) = 5th Floor
  2. Last two digits (03) = Room 3
  3. Building A, 5th Floor, Room 3

Newcomers

Tips & Survival Guide

Advice from seniors who've been there

✅ First Week Checklist

Day 1: Register at Student Services Office
Day 1: Get Student ID Card
Day 1: Tour the campus buildings
Day 2: Set up WiFi Account at IT Support Center
Day 2: Register with Campus Security (Dorm students)
Day 3: Attend your first classes
Day 3: Visit Library and get library card
Day 4: Open bank account (Off-campus students)
Day 4: Explore campus dining options
Day 5: Join a club or activity
Day 5: Make friends and find study partners

🎒 Essential Items to Bring

Documents:

  • ✓ Passport or National ID
  • ✓ Admission Letter
  • ✓ Health Insurance Card
  • ✓ Proof of Residence

For Class:

  • ✓ Laptop/Tablet (recommended)
  • ✓ Notebooks & Stationery
  • ✓ Calculator
  • ✓ Planner/Calendar

💰 Money-Saving Tips

  • ✓ Eat at main cafeteria (cheapest option)
  • ✓ Buy meal plan tickets (10–15% discount)
  • ✓ Use student discounts everywhere
  • ✓ Check for free campus events
  • ✓ Buy used textbooks from seniors
  • ✓ Rent textbooks instead of buying
  • ✓ Use library resources (free!)
  • ✓ Share transportation costs

🎓 What to Expect at University

  • ✓ More freedom in class scheduling
  • ✓ Bigger classes in first year
  • ✓ More independent study required
  • ✓ Group projects are common
  • ✓ Office hours with professors
  • ✓ Active club culture
  • ✓ Variety of learning activities
  • ✓ Opportunity to develop leadership skills

🆘 Where to Get Help

  • ✓ Academic Issues: Advisor or Department Office
  • ✓ Emotional Support: Counseling Center
  • ✓ Financial Aid: Student Services
  • ✓ Health Issues: Health Center
  • ✓ Technical Issues: IT Support
  • ✓ General Info: Student Services Desk

🌟 Campus Culture Tips

  • ✓ Be respectful to seniors
  • ✓ Join clubs and make friends
  • ✓ Participate in campus events
  • ✓ Respect campus quiet hours (after 22:00)
  • ✓ Use campus facilities responsibly
  • ✓ Attend academic seminars
  • ✓ Respect different cultures & backgrounds
  • ✓ Help younger students when possible

📜 Important Campus Rules

🏫 In Class

  • Arrive on time
  • Respect professors
  • No phones during lecture
  • Participate actively

📚 In Library

  • Keep quiet
  • Turn off phone
  • No eating (except water)
  • Return books on time

🏘️ In Dormitory

  • Quiet hours 22:00–08:00
  • No loud music
  • Guests by permission
  • Keep room clean

🚫 General

  • No smoking on campus
  • No alcohol in dorms
  • Respect property
  • Follow dress code
