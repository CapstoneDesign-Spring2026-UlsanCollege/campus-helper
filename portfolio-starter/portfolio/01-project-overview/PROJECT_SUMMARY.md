- **Team:** Campus Helper Team
- **Project:** Campus Helper
- **Team members:**

  - Bibek Kunwar

- **Target user:**
  Ulsan College students who need quick access to campus resources, study support, communication tools, and student services through a single platform.

- **Problem:**
  Students often use multiple platforms for notes sharing, campus announcements, buying and selling items, academic support, and communication. This creates confusion and makes it difficult to access important information efficiently.

- **Value statement:**
  Campus Helper provides a centralized platform where students can access campus information, share notes, use AI assistance, browse marketplace listings, and stay updated with official notices, all in one place.

- **Final MVP summary:**
  Campus Helper is a full-stack web application that includes user authentication, a student dashboard, notes sharing, a marketplace system, AI-powered assistance, admin notice management, and Like functionality for notes and marketplace posts. The platform is designed to improve communication, collaboration, and access to campus resources for students.
